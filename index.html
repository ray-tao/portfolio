about

hi! my name is ray and i love anything to do with art & design. born in connecticut and raised in hong kong, i'm shanghainese and speak fluent english and mandarin. currently, i'm majoring in communication design at parsons school of design NYC. through the many styles i do, i make art through various softwares like photoshop, illustrator, indesign, figma, HTML & CSS, autodesk maya, 3D printing, and laser cutting.
